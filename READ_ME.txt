Welcome to the Multithread Parser

- Press 0 to switch multithread/monothread, the background color should change (red = mono, blue= mutli)

- Press 1 to load the Locomotive Model

- Press 2 to load the Skull Model

- Press 3 to press the bunny model

- Press 4 to load the cube model

- Press 5 to load the Padoru model

- Press del to erase the scene and the previous load resources

- Press W to go forward

- Press S to gon backward

- Press A to go on the left

- Press D to go on the right

- Press Left Shift to sprint

- Press F to switch draw Fill/Line mode

Have fun :)
